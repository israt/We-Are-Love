Overview

Committed to provide support

Mission Statement

We R Love Foundation is committed to provide support to underprivileged youth throughout the world, wherever relief is needed. Making it a priority to reach out and support children in vulnerable communities in order to give them an opportunity to live a successful life is our objective. Through commitment and excellence, we will be the most efficient support organization. With contributions from members and donors, the disadvantaged youth will for once have an advantage.

Vision Statement

Reaching out, impacting, and supporting youth, communities and lives for a better future.

We R Love Foundation is a Non-Profit Organization that have a desirable vision to support underprivileged youth throughout the world by providing quality education, promote health awareness, providing clothes and all the necessities a child need. Investing our donations, time and volunteering efforts in our mission to improve the quality of life for every child is our goal. We believe the children are a symbol of hope for the future. This statement serves as a determination, motivation and an inspiration to those who share the same vision as We R Love Foundation.

Our Purpose

There are many children out there in third world countries who are not able to afford school education, clothing nor can they even afford food to eat. These are some of many situations in which We R Love Foundation has a reason for its existence, which is to help the youth in need. Through developing programs and providing meals to children around the world, the youth of today will have a better future ahead of them.
